Running Tips

What to wear:

All year - running shoes. Use the 'wet footprint' method to find which type of shoe you need. For example, if you over pronate, you will want a 'stability' shoe. I use asics, GT-1000 brand.

All year - socks! I recommend getting athletic socks. These will last longer and will add stability and comfort.

  • Spring (37 - 55 degrees)
    1. Long-sleeved technical tee.
    2. Capri-length running tights or running shorts.
    3. (Optional) Headband and/or gloves.
  • Summer (55 - 90 degrees)
    1. Short-sleeved technical tee OR tank.
    2. Shorts.
  • Fall (37 - 55 degrees)
    1. Short OR Long - sleeved technical tee.
    2. Capri-length running tights or running shorts.
    3. (Optional - end of season) Headband and/or gloves.
  • Winter (10 - 37 degrees)
    1. Long-sleeved technical tee and technical jacket.
    2. Long running tights.
    3. Hat/Headband and gloves.
    4. (Optional: under 10 degrees) Hoodie, double pants, double gloves.
